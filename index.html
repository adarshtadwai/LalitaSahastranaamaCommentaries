वस्तु स्तोतुं शिवाङ्कवास्तव्यम् ।
अस्तु चिरन्तनसुकृतैः
प्रस्तुतकाम्याय तन्मम पुरस्तात् ॥
अलमलमकुसुमबाणैः
अबिम्बशोणैरपुण्ड्रकोदण्डैः ।
अकुमुदबान्धवचूडैः
अन्यैरिह जगति दैवतं मन्यैः ॥
॥ सकलेष्टार्थदा देवी साधकस्तत्र दुर्लभः ॥
ललितासहस्रनामानि

About This Collection

This project aims to bring together comprehensive commentaries on the Lalita Sahasranama (Thousand Names of Goddess Lalita), a sacred text from the Brahmanda Purana containing 1000 names of the Divine Mother in Her aspect as Lalita Tripurasundari.

We are working to compile traditional Sanskrit commentaries by renowned scholars Bhāskarārāya (Soubhagya Bhaskara) and Bhaṭṭa Nārāyaṇa, along with simple English meanings for all 1000 names to make this sacred text accessible to devotees worldwide.

Features

  • Traditional Commentaries: Sanskrit commentaries from Bhāskarārāya and Bhaṭṭa Nārāyaṇa
  • English Meanings: Simple English meanings for all 1000 names, plus detailed commentary for 112+ names
  • Smart Navigation: Search & filter by Devanagari text or number, alphabetical sorting, collapsible filters
  • Direct Access: Clean URLs for individual names (e.g., /name/1/, /name/123/)
  • Responsive Design: Works seamlessly on desktop, tablet, and mobile devices
